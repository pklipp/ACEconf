From 1000 zł to 100 000 zł

with Nate Riks

My journey from starting a startup years ago that was making 1.000zl a month till the moment that I was making 100.000 a month.

Also there will be a part where he will show you how to validate your concept or startup idea and how lean startup changed a lot of companies. Secondly it will feature the methods used by Dropbox and Airbnb.
