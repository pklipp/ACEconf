Leadership is a choice, not a rank

with Bettina Shzu-Juraschek

Good people are waiting to be promoted to a leadership position, but did you know that you can promote yourself by simply choosing yourself to lead? As Simon Sinek says, "Leadership is a choice, not a rank."

Bettina's Bio:

By day Bettina Shzu-Juraschek is the product manager of a 7-figure business unit at Germany's leading networked marketplace for real estate, Immobilien Scout. By night she organizes free, all-volunteer coding workshops in Clojure, Rails, SQL, JavaScript, CSS & HTML to grow diversity in tech as well as coaches women to get tech jobs. As a product manager, workshop organizer, and coach, she brings together colleagues, volunteers, and peers to achieve results without having any power over any of them and even daring to have a little fun along the way. A native Texan Bettina has lived in Berlin since 2008.
