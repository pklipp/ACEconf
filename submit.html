Submit a Talk or Workshop to ACE! 2017

ACE! 2017 combines lean/agile and LeanUX/Lean Startup topics in two tracks of 45 minute talks and adds a workshop track for workshops of 1-2 hours in length. Appropriate topics include (but are not limited to):

  • Scrum
  • Kanban
  • Retrospectives
  • Lean startup
  • Lean UX
  • Design Thinking
  • Customer Development
  • Growth hacking
  • Collaboration and communication
  • Managing complexity
  • Quality assurance
  • Software craftsmanship
  • Communication and Collaboration
  • Usability and accessibility

We welcome speakers of any gender identity and expression, and regardless of sexual orientation.

We're also looking for a mix of experience reports and fresh ideas. If you're in doubt about whether your topic is applicable, please get in touch with paul@wawelhill.com.

If your talk is accepted, you can look forward to:

  • free admission to the conference
  • paid travel (up to 200 PLN for Polish speakers, 300 Euro for European speakers, and 1000 Euro for non-Europeans) if your travel is not paid by your employer
  • accommodation for up to three nights will be covered by the ACE! Conference or a sponsor if it is not paid by your employer
  • at least one speaker's dinner
  • If you're new to public speaking, we'll be happy to assist you with preparing your presentation, rehearsing it, and making your first speaking experience as successful as possible.
  • We are happy to accomodate the needs of speakers with disabilities and non-neurotypical cognitive styles. There's no need to specify your requirements in the form. If you're invited to speak based upon your proposed content, we can talk about how to make the experience comfortable and successful for you. The venue is wheelchair accessible.
  • and for the first time, all speakers will be compensated for their time. We're doing this to attract people who do not have the support of their employers but have stories and experiences to share. If your employer requires you to use holiday time or unpaid leave to speak at ACE!, you will be paid 450 Euro (gross) if your talk proposal is accepted. This figure is based on average IT salaries in Europe and America (and our budget).

As well as terrific conversations at speakers' dinners as well as during the conference, and lots of opportunities to interact with other speakers and attendees. ACE! is about connection and discovery, and we show our appreciation for your contribution by being the best hosts we can be and creating a terrific experience for our speakers. It will feel like a holiday in which you also learn and make new friends.

The way that we intend to cover the costs of travel, accommodation, and compensation is to require that every speaker be sponsored. The sponsor can be your own company, another company, or the conference organizers. We take responsibility for finding all sponsors, but if you're employed, we might ask for an introduction to someone in your marketing department.

By submitting the form below, you agree that Wawel Hill sp. z o.o., organizer of the ACE! Conference, may use the data that you submit for promoting the ACE! Conference and that, if accepted and invited to speak, your travel expenses, accomodation, meals and local transportation may be provided in full or in part by Wawel Hill sp. z o.o..

If filling in the form is difficult on the tool that you use to access this web page, you can email a description of your proposed talk to paul@wawelhill.com

The call for speakers will close on 15 January.
