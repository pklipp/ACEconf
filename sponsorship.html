About ACE!

Building Better Software. Building Software Better.

ACE! is the largest regional conference of its kind in Central Europe, attracting people from all over the region. With its startup climate, great universities, and large foreign investments, Krakow has become a major technology center in Central Europe. We expect to sell 300-400 tickets to ACE! 2015, mostly to managers, developers, and designers. Videos from past ACE! conferences were viewed almost 200,000 times in the past year!

Sponsor Speakers

VAT of 23% will be added when applicable. If you have questions about how to sponsor speakers, or if you have a suggestion for another way that your company can support ACE!, please get in touch. You may choose which speakers you would like to sponsor, or you can leave the decision to the organizers.

Your sponsorship of speakers will pay for their travel, accomodation, and speaking fee. All speakers at ACE! 2017 will be paid for their participation. Speaker sponsors will be listed with the speaker in the program as well as receive the following benefits: logo in the program and on the conference poster and beside your sponsored speaker on the posted agendas, mention in press releases, distribution of promotional material (1 pc. per 500 Euro), sponsors contributing over 1000 Euro will be entitled place a banner in the break area and sponsors contributing over 2000 Euro will be invited to install a booth in the venue and will have their logo included in the video placeholder (seen by thousands of people for years to come). The more speakers you sponsor, the more visibility your brand will receive.
